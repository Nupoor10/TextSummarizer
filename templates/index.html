HOW IT WORKS?

📌 Text Summarizer application is powered by Natural Language Processing

📌 Firstly, we use the NLTK toolkit to clean, process and tokenize our data

📌 Using the Frequency Method we find the frequency of all the words in our text data and store the text data and its frequency in a dictionary

📌 After that, we tokenize our text data. The sentences which contain more high frequency words will be kept in our final summary data.
